Stuffed Peppers

Description

This is an easy recipe to follow to make very tasty stuffed peppers! this recipe can be easily altered to your tastes.

Ingredients

  • Bell Peppers
  • Ground Beef
  • Rice
  • Cheese
  • Paparika
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the peppers for 5 minutes
  2. Cut the top of the peppers and scoop out the seeds
  3. Cook and season the ground beef with salt, pepper, and paparika
  4. Cook the Rice
  5. Combine rice and beef into a bowl
  6. Put rice/beef mixture into the bell pepper, topped with cheese
  7. Bake the pepper in the oven at 450f for 30 minutes or until cheese starts to brown
