Steak

Description

A great steak is an easy recipe to follow and only takes 5 minutes. Any cut of steak can be used but for this recipe I prefer eye round

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • pepper
  • salt>
  • garlic cloves
  • Thyme
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up pan
  2. Season the steak with salt and pepper to taste
  3. Melt butter into pan
  4. Place steaks into pan
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes
    6. \
  6. Flip steaks and add garlic cloves and thyme
  7. Baste steaks with excess butter
